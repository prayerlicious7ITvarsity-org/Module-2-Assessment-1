WARTHOG

The common warthog is a wild member of the pig family found in grassland, savanna, and woodland in sub-Saharan Africa. In the past, it was commonly treated as a subspecies of P. aethiopicus, but today that scientific name is restricted to the desert warthog of northern Kenya, Somalia, and eastern Ethiopia. Wikipedia

Trophic level: Herbivorous Encyclopedia of Life Scientific name: Phacochoerus africanus Family: Suidae Gestation period: 152 – 183 days Height: 64 – 85 cm (Adult, At Shoulder) Mass: Male: 60 – 150 kg (Adult), Female: 45 – 75 kg (Adult)
