GORILLA

Gorillas are ground-dwelling, predominantly herbivorous great apes that inhabit the tropical forests of central Sub-Saharan Africa. The genus Gorilla is divided into two species: the eastern gorillas and the western gorillas, and either four or five subspecies. They are the largest living primates. Wikipedia

Lifespan: 35 – 40 years Height: Western gorilla: 1,6 m Gestation period: Western gorilla: 257 days Encyclopedia of Life Scientific name: Gorilla Mass: Western gorilla: 160 kg, Eastern gorilla: 140 – 210 kg Speed: Western gorilla: 40 km/h, Eastern gorilla: 40 km/h
